Take your favorite websites on your e-Reader while traveling.

Just login with your Google account, add a few feeds, and get a direct link to get your feeds as ePub books.

You can as well go to http://letsread.me with your e-reader browser and directly get your ePub feeds downloaded.

The service has been developed with Chrome and Kobo Touch and is barely tested. This is just an idea quickly developed as a proof of concept. Please let me know if you want it to go further.
